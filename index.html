About HackBVP@4.0

Hackathon: Hack@BVP 4.0 is a 12-hour hackathon. Its mission is to provide a thrilling experience to students, developers, and anyone interesting in coding. Teams of developers, designers, and marketers will come together to build functioning apps, websites, programs or anything that tackle environmental issues in just 12 hours. Our theme is based on Issues like fit India, Anti plastic , tourism , population and traffic control. These topics are gaining a lot of much- needed attention and prompting discussions. At a local level, communities are addressing the issues of Plastic ban and population control, but we as students should contribute more towards it so let’s do something that makes difference in the society
Bring Yourself or Bring Your Team! Register your Team: https://forms.gle/iCbAL5GYjDEdhYWj9 Open to Professionals and Students, 18+
